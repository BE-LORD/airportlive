## POST-PROCESSING GUIDE
## ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Apply this Lightroom preset-style grade to ALL images for consistency:

```
Temp:           +15 (warmer)
Tint:           +5 (slight magenta)
Exposure:       -0.15 (slightly underexposed = premium)
Highlights:     -20 (recover blown highlights)
Shadows:        +10 (lift shadows slightly)
Whites:         -10
Blacks:         -15 (deeper blacks, not pure)
Clarity:        +5
Vibrance:       +8
Saturation:     -5 (slightly desaturated = editorial)

Split Toning:
Highlights:     Hue 40 (warm gold), Saturation 10
Shadows:        Hue 220 (cool blue), Saturation 5

Grain:          Amount 12, Size 30, Roughness 50
```

This creates the "SONA CORRIDOR" look — warm cream with earthy golds.
