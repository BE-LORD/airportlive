## 01 — TONE OF VOICE
## ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

### Brand Voice: Quietly Confident
V3 doesn't boast. It states. Like a five-star hotel that doesn't say "we are luxurious" — it just IS.

**Characteristics:**
- Precise (never vague — "95km in 90 minutes" not "fast travel")
- Warm (not corporate — "we'll be there when you land" not "arrival services available")
- Visual (sensory language — "leather seat", "cool cabin", "first ring")
- Earned confidence (no superlatives — "the best" "top rated" — just facts that sell themselves)
- Bilingual awareness (English primary, Hindi phrases welcome in accents)

**Copy that sounds like V3:**
> "Your flight lands. Your car is already waiting."
> "Punjab's most trusted road has a name now."
> "Book once. We handle the rest."

**Copy that does NOT sound like V3:**
> "We provide best-in-class airport transportation services"
> "Our team of professionals ensures your comfort"
> "Luxury travel at affordable prices"
